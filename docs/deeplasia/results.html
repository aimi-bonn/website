Deeplasia: prior-free deep learning for pediatric bone age assessment robust to skeletal dysplasias

Performance of Deeplasia for different skeletal disorders

The interactive figure below shows the comparison of the bone age predictions of Deeplasia (Rassmann et al. 2023) and the ground truth values (which are the average values of the two manual reference gradings on the German Dysplastic Bone Dataset). The dotted line denotes the mean deviation and the dashed lines the 95% confidence interval. Individual disorder categories are color-coded as indicated.

You can double-click on a disorder's name in the legend to isolate only that disorder in the plot.

Deeplasia was developed in the Artificial Intelligence in Medical Imaging (AIMI) group at the Institute for Genomic Statistics and Bioinformatics (IGSB) of the University of Bonn.
