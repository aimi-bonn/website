Prior-free deep learning for pediatric bone age assessment robust to skeletal dysplasias

Deeplasia achieves

  • State-of-the-art performance on the RSNA dataset (3.87 months MAD, n=200)
  • At least human-level performance on seven genetically-caused skeletal malformations (5.84 months MAD, n=568)
  • Validated for longitudinal monitoring of patients with genetic disorders (test-retest error of 2.74 months, n=149)

Deeplasia was developed in the Artificial Intelligence in Medical Imaging (AIMI) group at the Institute for Genomic Statistics and Bioinformatics (IGSB) of the University of Bonn.

Deeplasia is an open-source tool and the codes are accessible via the AIMI-Bonn GitHub.
